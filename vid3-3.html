What will you improve/innovate/upgrade from the system developed?

To expand the positive influence of AI on the lives of people experiencing serious health issues, it is essential to innovate and improve the system built for the "Healed Through AI" video.

First, the system can profit from the ongoing development of NLP and speech recognition software. It is crucial to enhance the precision and speed of speech recognition, especially for people with ALS like Tim Shaw. To achieve this goal, computers might be fine-tuned to recognize individual speech patterns, and customized language models could be provided to promote more fluent and natural conversation.
The second most important thing is making sure that everyone can use the system. The AI system could include support for other languages and dialects to meet the needs of a wider variety of users. Moreover, the whole user experience might be enhanced by making the UI more user-friendly and customizable, particularly for people of varying abilities.
Additionally, the system's adaptability can be increased with the addition of other assistive technologies and gadgets. Individuals like Dimitri Kanevsky may benefit from enhanced independence and productivity thanks to seamless integration with smart home devices, smartphone apps, and wearable technologies. Building reliable application programming interfaces and interoperability standards would be required.
The system's capabilities can also be enhanced by the current study of medical AI. Patients like Santhi would benefit immensely from the development of algorithms for early disease identification and progression tracking, since this would allow for more prompt intervention and improved disease management.
We must also place a premium on privacy and security. By bolstering data encryption and user data protection methods, we can keep the system secure and in line with privacy standards, which is crucial for gaining and keeping the confidence of our users.
Last but not least, iterative development and feedback from users is crucial. Continuous improvement activities can be steered by regularly requesting feedback from users, caregivers, and healthcare experts. Through this feedback loop, we can better understand user experience, fix problems, and adapt the system to match their changing requirements.
The system created for the "Healed Through AI" movie has already shown tremendous promise in improving the lives of people who are coping with health issues. We can continue to improve and upgrade the system to better assist and empower people like Tim Shaw, Dimitri Kanevsky, Santhi, and many more by concentrating on areas like accessibility, adaptability, medical AI, security, and user feedback. With these enhancements, AI will continue to serve as a source of light and healing for individuals in dark places.
